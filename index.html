About the Candidate
A Leader Built
for Kabwata

Clement Tembo is a dedicated community servant and proud son of Kabwata Constituency. Driven by a deep commitment to the people he grew up with, he has dedicated his career to uplifting livelihoods, championing accountability, and building bridges across communities.

Running under the United Party for National Development (UPND), Clement brings both passion and practical experience to the table. He understands the real challenges facing residents — from unemployment and poor infrastructure to limited access to quality healthcare and education.

His candidacy is not just a political campaign — it is a movement rooted in honesty, service, and results. Kabwata deserves a voice that speaks truth and delivers.
